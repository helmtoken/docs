# Principles

1. Immutable
2. Simple, low barrier to entry
3. Completely on-chain
4. Completely open source
5. Non-custodial
6. No counterparty risk
7. No initial supply
8. No team allocation
9. No management team
10. No investor allocation
11. No pre-mint
12. No centralization
13. No admin keys
14. Permissionless
15. Transparent
16. Trustless
17. Free mint
